Method. A scheduled job reads public social and news sources (Reddit finance subreddits, ApeWisdom aggregated mentions, StockTwits trending symbols, CNBC/MarketWatch/Yahoo Finance headlines) every ~12 hours, matches text against a fixed dictionary of theme keywords and representative tickers, and weights each match by engagement. Confidence = source diversity (40) + chatter dominance (30) + 1-month price confirmation of the theme's stock basket vs SPY (30). This is keyword-based listening, not human-level reading — it measures attention, which for momentum trading is the point, but it can misread sarcasm or novel phrasing. High social attention also often arrives after a move has started. Educational tool, not investment advice.
