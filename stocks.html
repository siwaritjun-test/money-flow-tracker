Method. Universe: ~8 largest/most liquid holdings of each SPDR sector ETF (88 stocks). The momentum playbook implemented here: (1) the regime bar says whether risk-taking is being rewarded at all; (2) sector money flow + RRG quadrant pick where to fish; (3) the composite score picks the strongest fish — relative strength vs its own sector, money-flow indicators (MFI, OBV trend, signed dollar volume), proximity to 52-week highs, volume confirmation and trend filters. Stops are suggested at 2×ATR(14) below price, with position size = (account × risk%) ÷ stop distance. Returns use dividend/split-adjusted closes. Data: Yahoo Finance daily bars via the repo's GitHub Action. Estimates, not actual fund flows; educational tool, not investment advice.
