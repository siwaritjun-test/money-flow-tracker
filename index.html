Methodology. True fund-flow data is proprietary, so flows are estimated: each asset's return over the period is compared with its group average; the excess return is multiplied by average daily dollar volume (capped at 2.5× group median to prevent one giant asset dominating). Negative scores are treated as money leaving, positive as money entering, and outflows are distributed to inflows pro-rata to build the flow map. Data: Yahoo Finance daily bars fetched live in your browser. This is an analytical visualization, not investment advice.
